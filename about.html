ABOUT

We are The Informative Four, a dedicated Filipino crime YouTube channel that specializes in shedding light on unsolved crimes in the Philippines. Through our engaging and informative content, we strive to bring attention to these unresolved cases, hoping to generate awareness for those who have long awaited justice. Our channel is committed to thorough research, responsible reporting, and creating a community that is passionate about seeking truth and resolution in these unsettling mysteries.

As The Informative Four, we aim to not only provide a platform for discussing unsolved crimes but also to foster a supportive community that advocates for justice. Our dedication extends beyond simply sharing stories; we actively collaborate with law enforcement authorities, legal experts, and relevant organizations to contribute to ongoing investigations and bring closure to affected individuals and families.

By creating a space where individuals can share information, provide support, and engage in constructive discussions, we strive to empower our audience to become proactive in seeking resolution for these cases. We are committed to upholding the highest ethical standards in our reporting, and our ultimate goal is to inspire positive change and contribute to the pursuit of justice in the Philippines.
