About Us

Hello! We are a group called 4 Sitio Bebe 2023, and our goal is to support the small town of Sitio Bebe. Due to their status as a small town, they have been neglected by government officials. They face various health issues and challenges, so we are committed to doing our best to support them.

Our team is comprised of dedicated individuals who share a common goal: to make a difference in the lives of Sitio Bebe's residents. We believe that everyone deserves access to basic necessities and healthcare, and we're taking action to make it a reality.

How We Help

We have planned a grand concert to raise awareness about Sitio Bebe. The concert features renowned Filipino artists who have generously offered their talents to support our cause. The funds generated from the event will be used to support various projects in the town.

Our concert is not just about music; it's about spreading awareness, fostering a sense of community, and making a positive impact. We believe that music has the power to inspire change, and we are harnessing that power for the benefit of Sitio Bebe.

Our Projects

Hydroponic Farming

Cost: 150-200k PHP

We aim to establish hydroponic farming in Sitio Bebe, providing a sustainable source of fresh produce and a potential income stream for the community. This project not only addresses food scarcity but also promotes self-sufficiency and environmental sustainability.

Medical Tent

Cost: 250k PHP (per event)

We plan to set up a medical tent at least once every two months for a year. Medical professionals will provide free healthcare services to the residents of Sitio Bebe. These events aim to address the immediate health needs of the community and promote regular check-ups and healthcare awareness.
